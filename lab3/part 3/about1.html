About Me - Page 1

Hello everyone! On this first page, I want to share the games I'm currently playing and a brief description of each. The first game series I'll talk about is from Hoyoverse, a Chinese game company that started with a 2D game called GGZ and evolved into globally popular games like Honkai Impact 3, Genshin Impact, Honkai Star Rail, and Zenless Zone Zero.

The first game, Honkai Impact 3, is an action gacha game that I started playing as a kid. It was incredibly fun, especially the thrill of pulling characters, building teams, and dealing millions of damage. To this day, I still enjoy playing it.
